Mental Health

Mental health is the emotional, psychological, and social well-being of a person.
It's extremely important. It affects people's actions, emotions, and even relationships with people. A positive mindset
allows people to handle their stress and be productive. In the United States, almost 1 in 5 experience a mental health problem each year. Many people have more than one mental disorder. Common mental health issues include anxiety, depression, mood disorders, and schizophrenia.

Signs of Mental Illness:

  • Eating too little or too much
  • Sleeping too little or too much
  • Low energy
  • Having feelings of hoplessness
  • Feeling unusually confused, angry, upset, sad, scared, or worried
  • Thoughts of self-harm

How to keep a positive mindset:

  • Seek professional help!
  • Being physically active!
  • Getting enough sleep (8+ hours)
  • Talk to a trusted friend or family member

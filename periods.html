Periods

Life Hacks.

The app “Clue” is great for tracking your period.

Buy hand warmers from walmart or local store and stick them inside the waistband of your pants so you have a heating pad on the go!

Drink lots of water to stay hydrated!

DO NOT DRINK COFFEE! Coffee just makes cramps even worse and thats never good. Instead try for an alternative like tea!

If you're in the bathroom and dont want to make too much noise when you open a pad, cut off the sides like the frills and you wont make as much noise!

A period kit should be a must. Carry one in your locker or purse.

Things it might include:

- pads or tampons - advil or any medication you take. - an extra pair of - an extra pair of underwear

Periods

What is a period?

It is when blood and tissue from your uterus comes out of your vagina. It usually happens every month.

What is a Menstrual Cycle?

Your menstrual cycle helps your body prepare for pregnancy every month. It also makes you have a period if you’re not pregnant.

Life Hacks.

The app “Clue” is great for tracking your period.

Buy hand warmers from Walmart or local store ,and stick them inside the waistband of your pants so you have a heating pad on the go!

Drink lots of water to stay hydrated!

DO NOT DRINK COFFEE! Coffee just makes cramps even worse a,nd thats never good. Instead try for an alternative like tea!

If you're in the bathroom ,and dont want to make too much noise. When you open the pad cut off the frilly part on the sides ,and you wont make as much noise.

A period survival kit should be a must. Keep one in your locker or purse.

Things it might include:

- pads or tampons - advil or any medication you take. - hand sanitizer - mini snacks - an extra pair of underwear

Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment includes requests for sexual favors, advances, or other conducts either through physical or verbal action. It can also include offensive remarks about someone’s gender.

Sexual harassment in the workplace: 1 in 3 women report that they have been sexually harassed in their workplace. Report any cases and write down the situation exactly as it happened. Talk to someone you can trust. If it doesn’t get better, look for a different job.

How to cope:

  • It’s not your fault!

  • Think positively!

  • Do things you have always enjoyed

  • Take your time

  • Talk to a trusted friend

  • Seek help
